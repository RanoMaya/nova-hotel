About

Nova Sky is an unparalleled, visionary lifestyle Hotel, located in a spectacular setting, along the Northern City belt of Erbil. As an upmarket Kurdistan Hotel, Nova Sky stands as the getaway.

Hotel Nova Sky combines the elegance of contemporary architecture with luxurious furnishings and sophistication, offering the ultimate in privacy, security, connoisseurial cuisine and unparalleled service.

Nova Sky comprises 67 rooms, with a rare combination of splendor and the ultimate in comfort. Guests could reach the hotel within 5 mins from the Erbil International airport
